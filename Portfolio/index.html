About me

Naima worked with a health organization in Lamu conducting research on antenatal care, during this period she noticed a gap in pre and postnatal care and the rising cases of child mortality. These challenges continue to affect her community; she believes that with her background in software development and her desire to give back to her community she is in a position to use technology to address inequalities in health care.
